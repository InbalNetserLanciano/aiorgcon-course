# From AI Tools to Organizational Transformation — Commercial Script
### The 3I Framework for HR AI Implementation

**Format:** 9 scenes / ~90–120 seconds  
**Concept:** A structured, cinematic brand film positioning the 3I Framework as the definitive path from scattered AI tool use to full organizational transformation — built for HR and C-suite audiences.  
**Tone:** Energetic, authoritative, slightly futuristic. Realistic people and environments layered with high-tech overlays. Ambitious but grounded.  
**Brand:** People Tech Consulting Solutions / Inbal Netser Lanciano  
**Color palette:** Deep purple `#500B75` · Turquoise `#0BFCCE` · Clean white · Dark navy · Glassmorphism overlays

**Inspiration references:**
- *Atera B2B AI Campaign* (2024) — end-to-end AI-generated workplace ad; energetic pacing, futuristic overlays on real work environments, built with Runway + ElevenLabs
- *Workday "Work Enters a New Era"* — enterprise HR + AI positioning, aspirational tone, human-agent collaboration
- *IBM "AI in the Workplace"* — thought-leadership narrative structure, problem → framework → transformation arc
- *Microsoft × Workday Agentic AI* — platform thinking, human + AI as one integrated system, clean visual language

---

## VISUAL DIRECTION

**Vibe:** Energetic, realistic, modern, slightly futuristic, high-tech organizational environment.

**Visual language:** Real people, real offices, real HR and leadership situations — combined with subtle futuristic overlays: glowing data lines, transparent dashboards, AI workflow layers, purple and turquoise motion accents.

**Logo integration:** Animated MP4 logo used as a premium brand transition — not only as a static ending.

| Placement | Timing |
|---|---|
| Opening micro-brand moment | 1 second logo animation before Scene 1 |
| Corner watermark | Small semi-transparent logo, top-right, Scenes 4–8 |
| Framework reveal | Logo appears next to "The 3I Framework" title in Scene 4 |
| Final brand lockup | Full MP4 logo animation integrated with closing line |

## SCENES

---

### SCENE 0 — Brand Pulse Opening
**Duration:** 1–2 seconds  
**Camera:** Static shot  
**Location:** Pure black / brand environment  
**Action:** Animated MP4 logo appears with a sharp, energetic motion. Purple and turquoise light streaks move across the screen. Fast digital pulse. Clean high-tech transition into Scene 1.  
**On-screen text:**
> People Tech Consulting Solutions
> *or*
> Inbal Netser Lanciano

**Voiceover:** None

---

### SCENE 1 — AI Is Already Here
**Duration:** 5 seconds  
**Camera:** Slow dolly (moving through the workspace)  
**Location:** Open-plan modern office — employees at screens, in meetings, reviewing documents  
**Action:** Fast realistic cuts. Employees using AI in documents, meetings, dashboards, presentations, and search. Screens move quickly. Subtle AI interface overlays appear on top of real work environments.  
**Logo:** None, or very subtle watermark only after the first 2 seconds.  
**On-screen text:**
> AI is already inside the organization.

**Voiceover:**
> AI is already changing the way people work.
> In meetings. In documents. In analysis. In decision making.
> In the way people search, write, plan and create.
>
> But scattered use is not transformation.

---

### SCENE 2 — The Strategic Decision
**Duration:** 5 seconds  
**Camera:** Push-in (slow push toward the leadership table)  
**Location:** Modern boardroom — CEO and CHRO in a decisive meeting  
**Action:** CEO and CHRO at a modern leadership meeting. On screen: organizational goals — productivity, innovation, employee experience, skills, growth. They look decisive, not confused. They mark AI as a strategic lever on the organizational roadmap.  
**Logo:** Small logo watermark appears subtly in the corner.  
**On-screen text:**
> The CEO and CHRO decide:
> AI must support organizational goals.

**Voiceover:**
> Then comes the strategic decision.
>
> The CEO and CHRO decide to harness AI — not as a trend, and not as another technology project — but as a lever for achieving organizational goals.
>
> Improving productivity. Strengthening decision making. Developing people. Enhancing employee experience. Building new capabilities. Creating measurable business value.
>
> And this is where the real work begins.

---

### SCENE 3 — The Real Challenge
**Duration:** 5 seconds  
**Camera:** Slow dolly (lateral movement through the space)  
**Location:** Mixed HR / management environment — teams, whiteboards, screens  
**Action:** The leadership decision moves into the organization. HR, managers and employees face practical questions. The pace is energetic. Floating keywords appear as dynamic data layers: **Privacy · Skills · Adoption · Data · Governance · Trust · Workflows · Measurement**  
**Logo:** Small watermark remains, not competing with the keywords.  
**On-screen text:**
> The challenge is not choosing a tool.
> The challenge is designing the work.

**Voiceover:**
> Because the real challenge is not choosing another AI tool.
>
> The real challenge is translating strategy into a new way of working.
>
> What should we automate? Where do we need human judgment?
> How do we build trust? How do we redesign processes?
> How do we turn experimentation into measurable value?

---

### SCENE 4 — The Framework Reveal
**Duration:** 5 seconds  
**Camera:** Static shot  
**Location:** Abstract branded environment — visual noise organizing into structure  
**Action:** Visual noise resolves into a clean three-level architecture. Three glowing layers materialize: **Improve · Integrate · Impact**. The animated logo appears briefly beside the framework title, as if it "activates" the model. After the animation, a small static logo remains next to the title or in the corner.  
**Logo:** Short segment of the MP4 logo animation integrated into the reveal. Static logo remains after.  
**On-screen text:**
> The 3I Framework
> for HR AI Implementation
>
> IMPROVE
> INTEGRATE
> IMPACT

**Voiceover:**
> This is where AI implementation becomes organizational design.
>
> The 3I Framework for HR AI Implementation creates a structured path from AI tools to organizational transformation.
>
> Three levels. One connected system.

---

### SCENE 5 — Level 1: Improve
**Duration:** 5 seconds  
**Camera:** Push-in (slow push toward the individual at their screen)  
**Location:** Individual workspace — HR professional or employee at a realistic desk  
**Action:** Employee using AI to summarize, draft, analyze, prepare, compare and learn. Realistic workspace with subtle AI glow around documents and screens.  
**Logo:** Small watermark in the corner.  
**On-screen text:**
> 1. IMPROVE
> Individual and Functional Work

**Voiceover:**
> Improve is the first level.
>
> It focuses on individual and functional work.
>
> At this level, AI reduces time to output, improves quality, supports learning and increases personal agency.
>
> People move from uncertainty to hands-on experimentation.

---

### SCENE 6 — Level 2: Integrate
**Duration:** 5 seconds  
**Camera:** Slow dolly (moving across the team collaboration space)  
**Location:** Team workshop space — sticky notes, collaborative screens, workflow mapping  
**Action:** A team mapping a workflow. Sticky notes become a digital process map. Human checkpoints appear between AI-supported steps. The energy is collaborative and constructive.  
**Logo:** Small watermark in the corner.  
**On-screen text:**
> 2. INTEGRATE
> Workflows, Teams and Processes

**Voiceover:**
> Integrate is the second level.
>
> This is where AI moves from personal productivity into team workflows and HR processes.
>
> Recruitment. Learning. Performance management. Employee listening. Managerial routines.
>
> At this level, the organization redesigns how work actually happens.

---

### SCENE 7 — Level 3: Impact
**Duration:** 5 seconds  
**Camera:** Static shot (wide, commanding angle)  
**Location:** Executive environment — strategy table, HR dashboard, architecture map  
**Action:** Executive table, HR dashboard, strategy map, goals, people data and technology architecture connecting together. The connection between business strategy and human systems is visible. The look is ambitious, strategic, high-level.  
**Logo:** Logo watermark becomes slightly more visible as the story reaches strategic altitude.  
**On-screen text:**
> 3. IMPACT
> Strategy, Governance and Business Value

**Voiceover:**
> Impact is the third level.
>
> This is where AI connects to strategy, governance, measurement and business outcomes.
>
> AI becomes part of how the organization makes decisions, develops people, manages change and achieves goals.

---

### SCENE 8 — Closing: The New Architecture for Work
**Duration:** 5 seconds  
**Camera:** Push-in (slow push toward the unified system visual)  
**Location:** Branded abstract environment — four elements connecting into one  
**Action:** Four elements converge into one integrated system: **People · Data · Technology · Goals**. The 3I Framework appears clearly in branded purple and turquoise. Finish with the full animated MP4 logo as a final brand lockup.  
**Logo:** Full animated MP4 logo as the final visual signature.  
**On-screen text:**
> People × Data × Technology × Goals
>
> From AI Tools
> to Organizational Transformation
>
> The 3I Framework
> for HR AI Implementation

**Voiceover:**
> AI implementation is not a technology project.
>
> It is a new architecture for work.
>
> A way of working that connects people, data, technology and organizational goals. Not as separate initiatives. As one integrated system.
>
> The question is not whether AI will change work.
>
> The question is: who is designing the way it happens?
