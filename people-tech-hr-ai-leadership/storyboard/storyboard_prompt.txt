Cinematic storyboard for a 28-second commercial on AI transformation in organizations.

Create a 2x3 grid of 6 cinematic panels — 2 columns, 3 rows — each depicting a distinct scene. Each panel should be a photorealistic cinematic still with dramatic lighting, deep colors, and professional corporate cinematography. No text, no captions, no graphic overlays, no titles, no labels in any panel.

PANEL 1 (top-left): Open-plan modern office viewed from a wide angle. Three clearly separate clusters of workers visible — one cluster of developers on one side, a marketing team in the middle, an HR professional on the other side. Every screen glows with a different interface. People are busy and productive but completely disconnected from each other. Mood: fragmented, siloed, the new kind of organizational chaos. Cinematic wide shot, cool blue and purple office lighting, slight desaturation to emphasize disconnection.

PANEL 2 (top-right): Close medium shot of a professional woman (HR manager, 35-45, confident) at her desk, leaning forward toward her laptop with focused intensity. Her expression shows concentration and a small moment of discovery — she is testing something, experimenting, iterating. The screen reflects in her glasses. The environment is minimal and clean. Mood: individual mastery, the first step of fluency, genuine practice not passive learning. Warm focused light on her face, slightly darker background.

PANEL 3 (middle-left): A cross-functional working session in a modern meeting room. HR professional stands at a large whiteboard while seated around the table are people from clearly different departments — business casual mix. The whiteboard has a complex process diagram with some areas highlighted. People are engaged, leaning in, actively contributing. Mood: alignment happening in real time, silos breaking, HR as convener. Dynamic lighting, energy in the room, slightly elevated angle to show the whole group.

PANEL 4 (middle-right): C-suite boardroom. HR leader standing confidently at the head of a long table, not to the side — at the head. Behind her, a large screen shows an organizational chart being redesigned with new role titles. Around the table: CEO, CFO, and department heads are listening attentively. The HR leader commands the room. Mood: strategic authority, HR reshaping the future of work at the highest level. Dramatic cinematic lighting, deep shadows, powerful composition.

PANEL 5 (bottom-left): Modern office space — but redesigned and purposeful. A group of professionals move with clarity and intent. One person coordinates a dashboard showing AI agent outputs alongside human team metrics. A glass wall in the background has a revised organizational structure posted on it, with new role categories visible. The overall feeling is architecture — human and AI working in structured harmony. Mood: the organization after the transformation, clarity, alignment, new era. Slightly warmer lighting than Panel 1, more cohesion visually.

PANEL 6 (bottom-right): Dramatic minimal close-up. A single professional woman (same HR leader from Panels 2 and 4) looks directly into the camera with quiet authority and complete confidence. Dark background behind her. Just slightly lit from one side — cinematic portrait lighting. She is not presenting, not explaining — she is simply certain. Mood: this is the invitation, the direct contact moment, the professional who has the answer. High contrast, powerful, still.
